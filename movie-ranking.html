Christina's All Time Favorite Movies

Some Must-Sees

Wild

/>

Starring Reese Witherspoon, and based on the book, Wild, by Cheryl Strayed, A woman overcomes many difficulties she faced in her life, as well as terrible decisions she made in her past, by setting off on a journey to hike the Pacific Crest Trail. This movie is based on a true story.

The Dawn Wall

/>

Starring professional rock climbers, Tommy Caldwell and Kevin Jorgesson, 2 men work tirelessly for years to send what seems to be an impossible pitch on Yosemite National Park's famous El Capitan. The documentary largely follows the life of Tommy Caldwell and what inspired him to conquer such a seemingly impossible climb.

Funny Farm


Now, here is an oldie but goodie. Starring Chevy Chase back in the 80's, a man moves from the big city to a small country town by the name of RedBud. From a belligerent mailman, to finding a corpse buried in their garden, Andy and his wife, Elisabeth had no idea what they were signing up for when they decided to put roots down in this wayward town. This is Chevy at his finest, so if you haven't seen Funny Farm and you like to laugh, it is an absolute must.
